Boulder League

Feb 20th - March 13

This is a fun, relative comp where everyone’s hardest climb (within 3 attempts) earns the same points, regardless of difficulty. Don’t worry about holding your team back — just show up each week and have fun! Sign up in groups of four.This event is like a more formal version of the leaderboard you see in the front of the gym, where you will be competing over the course of the month.
